Thank you for choosing to support Food Donate Foundation. All 80G certificates (tax receipts) are processed within 10 working days of the donation date.

All donations are used towards supporting the overall operations of Zomato Feeding India across different programs. In light of the recent lockdowns caused by COVID-19, your donations to 'Feed the daily wager' will be utilised to ensure a reliable supply of meals to the daily wager community.

To donate excess food from such events, please fill the query form showcased below the FAQs and we'll get in touch with you. Request you to fill the form 15 days prior to the event for us to ensure timely pick ups.

We provide raw grains as well as cooked food depending on your requirement.
